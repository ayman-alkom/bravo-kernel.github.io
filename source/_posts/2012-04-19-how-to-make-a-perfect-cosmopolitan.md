Recipy for the perfect Cosmopolitan:

    2 parts good vodka
    1 part lightly sweetened cranberry juice
    1 part fresh lime juice

1. Put in a cocktail-shaker
2. Add hand-crushed ice
3. Shake the lot
4. Pour into two nice glasses rimmed with brown sugar
